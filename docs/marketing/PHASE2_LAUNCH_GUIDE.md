## Platform-Specific Tips

### GitHub Discussions ⭐
- **Where**: https://github.com/[org]/[repo]/discussions
- **Time to post**: 15 minutes
- **Engagement level**: High (technical audience)
- **Best response time**: Within 2 hours
- **Success**: 5-10 comments = great!

### Twitter/X 📱
- **Where**: https://twitter.com
- **Time to post**: 10 minutes per thread
- **Engagement level**: Medium-High
- **Best time**: 9-11 AM PT, 1-3 PM PT
- **Success**: 200+ impressions = good!

### Reddit 🔗
- **Where**: r/Python, r/MachineLearning, r/learnprogramming
- **Time to post**: 15 minutes
- **Engagement level**: High but community-dependent
- **Approval**: Some communities require mod approval
- **Success**: 50+ upvotes = great!

### Dev.to 📝
- **Where**: https://dev.to
- **Time to post**: 20 minutes
- **Engagement level**: Medium-High
- **Best content**: Tutorials, how-tos
- **Success**: 500+ views = good!

### Medium ✍️
- **Where**: https://medium.com
- **Time to post**: 20 minutes
- **Engagement level**: Medium
- **Best for**: Thought leadership, deep dives
- **Success**: 300+ views = good!

### Hashnode 🌐
- **Where**: https://hashnode.com
- **Time to post**: 20 minutes
- **Engagement level**: Medium
- **Best for**: Dev tutorials, career content
- **Success**: 200+ views = good!

## Troubleshooting Quick Guide

### "Nothing's happening - no comments"
✅ **Normal!** Most communities take 12-24 hours to see engagement
✅ Keep posting good content
✅ Engage in other communities to build visibility

### "Got a negative comment"
✅ Take 5 minutes, don't respond immediately
✅ Thank them for feedback
✅ Use response template from PHASE2_ENGAGEMENT_PLAYBOOK.md
✅ Most people will respect professional response

### "Post got rejected on Reddit"
✅ Check subreddit rules (you may have missed something)
✅ Try different subreddit
✅ Edit post and resubmit with corrections

### "Feeling overwhelmed"
✅ Follow PHASE2_CALENDAR.md - do ONE task at a time
✅ Start with 15 minutes
✅ You don't have to do everything at once
✅ Weekly review helps you see progress

### "How do I know if it's working?"
✅ Check PHASE2_CALENDAR.md success metrics
✅ Track daily in spreadsheet
✅ Compare to targets (not perfect targets, but direction)
✅ Look at cumulative progress over weeks
