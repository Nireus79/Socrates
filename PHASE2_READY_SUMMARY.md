## Key Success Factors

### What Will Drive Success

1. **Authenticity**: Real experiences, not hype
2. **Consistency**: Daily engagement, visible presence
3. **Value**: Help others, answer questions fully
4. **Patience**: Community builds over weeks, not days
5. **Quality**: Well-written posts, genuine responses
6. **Timing**: Post when communities are active

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid

❌ Posting at wrong times (late night/weekends)
❌ Spam-like behavior (too many posts, no engagement)
❌ Ignoring questions (hurts credibility)
❌ Hard selling (people want education, not ads)
❌ Giving up too early (week 1 is slow, week 3+ accelerates)
